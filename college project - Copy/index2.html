Courses We Offer

The BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application) - Vocational Course has been started in our L.N.T.College since 2015. This course curriculum is as per the guidelines of B.R.A. Bihar University Muzaffarpur. This course is a three year degree course and AICTE approved course in which students have to pass out 30 papers in five semesters & Sixth(last) semester is for Project Work and Seminar & Presentation. It is a very balanced course in terms of Practical and Theory papers, to develop a student for their skill and knowledge as per the market scenario.
